Svetlana Gruzinova

CPA, MAcc, Founder Honest Leading Inc.

Svetlana (‘Lana’) Gruzinova is a CPA and former Big 4 Deloitte Audit Manager who holds both Bachelors and Masters of Accountancy degrees. During 8 years with Deloitte, Lana worked with companies in a wide variety of industries including insurance (MetLife) and asset management (BlackRock and Merrill Lynch). Other industries served included consumer products, energy, and chemicals products. As part of her experience in the Deloitte Management Development Program, Lana led projects to develop content to support audit practitioners performing PCAOB audits across the globe in conjunction with performing local statutory audits based on ISA/AICPA standards. Lana managed the global team and her excellent project management skills, combined with strong knowledge of professional standards, prior auditing experience, dedication, and leadership enabled her to successfully complete the deployment of the new content according to tight timeline. Her presentation and public speaking experience enabled the project to get through design and final approvals by major Deloitte member firms’ leadership.

After 8 years with the Firm, Lana joined Mettler Toledo’s Product Inspection Division in Tampa as Assistant Controller. Her main responsibilities include financial reporting & analysis, forecasting, budgeting, adherence to SOX & coordination of internal/external audits.

In her spare time likes to spend time with her two daughters teaching them sports and Russian language and taking them to local Florida attractions.

Lana is actively involved in the local Tampa network of business leaders. She is also an author and currently publishes articles on LinkedIn.
