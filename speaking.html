What does it take to be an effective Modern Leader?

According to Emplify study (Emplify_Employee_Engagement_Trends_Report_2018), 75% have “moderately engaged” workforces. Are you the leader that is ready to change statistics? Then Leadership Summit Series is for you.

Modern leadership is no longer about engaging and motivating your team. This is what leaders have been taught and measured on. How engaged is your team? How do you motivate your team? These are terms from the past.

To change statistics, we need to find friendships and values: putting purpose behind work. We need to be engaging across ages and tenures. We need to help employees find meaning in work. Simply put, we need to inspire!

Program Benefits

  • Be inspired and motivated by great speakers.
  • Use specific takeaways that, if used properly, can bring you immediate benefits.
  • Interact with experienced facilitators who guide you through leadership principles, bring business lessons to life and help you adapt the concepts to your own professional development.
  • Network with like-minded professionals at entertaining and engaging celebrations.
