Can You Spot Uniqueness?

— May 22, 2019 —

Stones. Do they all look the same? How can you find the uniqueness of each one?

That’s what good leaders are encouraged to do. What are the unique skills your team has? Initially, all stones appear gray. And, yes, the majority are gray. Yet, after a thorough study, you may notice crystals, copper-colored stones, and other various shades in between.

A leader is responsible to understand what variety of stones you have on your team. Understand and provide what is needed to help these stones display their best traits.
